Student Life Centre (SLC) Info Desk

SLC Info Desk Staff

The information desk staff at the Doon campus will help you:

  • Book meeting rooms (please note: a valid Conestoga ONE Card is required)
  • Get directions and information about the Student Life Centre, including event bookings
  • Get contact and location information for Conestoga faculty and staff
  • Review the information playing on the LCD screens
  • View the Grand River Transit board and Conestoga campus maps

Drop by the Student Life Centre Info Desk by Door 3 (Tim Hortons) at the Doon campus or call us at 748-5220 ext. 2948 for assistance.
